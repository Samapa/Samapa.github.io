Redcity Roar Rebrand

About the Project

The client had a logo and uniform desin already but wanted them to be refreshed. I started with the logo, cleaning it up but still keeping close to the original design.

I used the logo colors to redesign their uniform. Redcity required a light, dark and home uniform to submit for approvals.

To give the client more inspiration I created three social cards and a coaches polo.
